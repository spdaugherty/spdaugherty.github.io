RESEARCH INTERESTS

I am interested in enumerative and algebraic combinatorics. I am interested in symmetric functions, the q,t-Catalan, numbers, combinatorial Hopf algebras, quasisymmetric and noncommutative symmetric functions, combinatorial statistics, chromatic symmetric functions, Tamari lattice generalizations, and parking functions.

I am a Burnett Meyer Postdoctoral Fellow under the supervision of Nat Thiem at the University of Colorado Boulder. I completed my PhD at North Carolina State University in 2024, advised by Laura Colmenarejo (NCSU) and Sarah Mason (WFU).
