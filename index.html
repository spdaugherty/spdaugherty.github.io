RESEARCH INTERESTS

I am interested in enumerative and algebraic combinatorics. My advisors are Laura Colmenarejo (NCSU) and Sarah Mason (WFU).

I am currently studying Schur-like bases, partially commutative generalizations of QSym and NSym, chromatic symmetric functions, and q,t-catalan numbers.
